Needs
Things you'd still need even on a tight month — school printing, transport/fare, load or data, and other unavoidable school costs. Since your parents already cover food, this bucket stays light for you — mainly transportation, load, and school supplies.
Wants
Guilt-free spending money — Valorant skins, hangouts, food trips with friends, games, gadgets you don't strictly need. This is the bucket to spend from freely without feeling bad, since it's already been set aside for exactly that.
Savings
Untouched money. No "spend from savings" option exists in this app on purpose — treat it as if it doesn't exist for everyday spending. Let it build up for something bigger later (new gear, emergencies, or just a growing cushion).
Rule of thumb: if it's something you need to function (school, transport), it's Needs. If it's something you'd enjoy but could skip, it's Wants. If you're not touching it any time soon, it's Savings.
